Bold & Italic

This section we will examine the meaning of bold and italics.

This is bold text , which is normally used to emphasise that this text is important. There are two ways to do this, using a < b > , but this option is not recommended, mainly because screen readers will ignore it. The best way is to use the tag < strong >, which will make your website more accessible.

Now this is a cursive text, as said in the previous text, there are two ways, the < i > , which is the easy way, just visual, and the < em > , which is the right way, to remind that this is not just a text thing, is important to help people who use a screen reader.
